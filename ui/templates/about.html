About Cawler ID

What It Does

Cawler ID is a web app that identifies bird species from audio recordings uploaded by the user.

How It Works

We convert your audio into a spectrogram, extract frequency‑domain features, and compare them to a dataset of known species. The system returns the closest matches based on similarity scoring and spectral shape alignment.
